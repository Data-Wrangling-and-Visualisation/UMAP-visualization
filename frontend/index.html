UMAP Visualization

A Powerful tool to see the process of fitting UMAP embeddings

About This Application

UMAP (Uniform Manifold Approximation and Projection) is a state-of-the-art dimensionality reduction technique that preserves both local and global structures in your data. It projects high-dimensional datasets into 2D or 3D space, making it easy to spot clusters, patterns, and outliers.

Why Use UMAP?

UMAP uncovers the underlying topology of your data, helping with visual exploration, cluster analysis, and anomaly detection in machine learning workflows.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Upload a CSV file containing your dataset. To color-code points by category, include a label column.
  2. Wait a few seconds for UMAP to process the data on the server.
  3. A 3D scatter plot will appear. Rotate, zoom, and pan to explore your embedding.
  4. Use the epoch slider below to visualize the fitting process step by step.
